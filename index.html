Essay Studio & AI Integrity FAQ

Does LaunchPath AI write essays for students?

No. Students provide their own ideas, stories, and drafts. LaunchPath AI acts as a coach — helping with structure, clarity, and grammar — not as a ghostwriter.

What does the AI actually help with?

It helps students brainstorm topics, organize outlines, improve wording, and suggest edits while keeping their voice at the center of every essay.

How do you make sure the essay still sounds like the student?

We encourage students to write multiple samples so the system can maintain consistent tone and flag sections that feel overly advanced or off-voice.

Is this allowed by colleges?

Yes. LaunchPath AI is designed as an assistive tool — similar to a writing tutor or Grammarly. Students remain the authors of their essays.

What happens to my child's data and essays?

Essays and student data stay private within LaunchPath AI. We do not share essays with colleges, do not sell student data, and do not use individual essays to train AI models.

Is this fair to students who don't use it?

LaunchPath AI is designed to level the playing field — giving every student access to the kind of guidance that currently costs $150–$300 per hour from a private counselor. Students remain the authors of their work.

Can counselors and parents feel confident about integrity?

Yes. The platform is built to support honest storytelling, not replace it. We focus on teaching stronger writing skills, not generating generic essays.
